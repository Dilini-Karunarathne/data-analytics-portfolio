# data-analytics-portfolio
Power BI dashboards for data analytics portfolio

# Project Overview

This repository showcases a collection of 4 end-to-end Power BI dashboards developed for business intelligence, data analytics, and performance reporting.
The project demonstrates practical skills in:

Data analysis and visualization
KPI development and business reporting
Data modeling (Star Schema design)
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) calculations
Interactive dashboard development for decision-making


# Sales Dashboard (Business KPI Reporting)

Designed for sales analytics and revenue tracking
Measures: Total Sales, Profit, Order Volume, Growth Trends
Identifies top-performing products and categories
Supports data-driven business decision-making

# HR Analytics Dashboard & HR OLAP Analytics Sashboard (Customer Segmentation & Behavior Analysis)

Performs customer segmentation and behavioral analysis
Identifies purchasing patterns and customer trends
Supports CRM and marketing strategy optimization
Enhances customer retention insights

# Sales & Risk Risk Analysis Dashboard (DAX-Based Risk Analysis)

Built using advanced DAX measures for risk classification
Identifies loss-making orders and high-risk transactions
Risk levels: Low, Medium, High, Critical
Supports operational risk management and business control
